# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Gym owners (single location to large multi-branch chains), personal trainers, and studio owners (pilates, yoga, crossfit, functional). Spanish-speaking market, primarily Uruguay and Argentina. They manage their business day-to-day: signing up members, scheduling classes, collecting payments, and handling client communication. They are not technical users — they value tools that just work.

## Product Purpose

MasSocios is a gym and fitness business management platform that handles subscriptions, class scheduling, online payments, client communication (via WhatsApp AI assistant), and business analytics. It targets everything from a solo personal trainer to a multi-branch franchise. Success means: fewer admin hours per week, more clients converted, fewer unpaid memberships, and a single place to see the health of the business.

## Brand Personality

Direct, confident, results-oriented. The brand speaks like a reliable partner — not a hype-driven startup, not a corporate enterprise tool. Three words: effective, practical, trustworthy.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS cream/sand aesthetics (too AI-generated looking)
- Overly playful or youth-oriented brands (this is a business tool for owners)
- Corporate enterprise software with stiff, formal design
- Health/wellness brands that feel spa-like or calm (this is about growth and control, not relaxation)

## Design Principles

1. **Show the outcome, not the feature** — Every section should answer "what does this get me?" not "what does this do?"
2. **Confidence over cleverness** — Direct headlines, clear numbers, no metaphors that need decoding
3. **Built for Latin American business owners** — Language, pricing in UYU, WhatsApp as primary channel — these are not afterthoughts
4. **Scale without complexity** — The product serves 1 gym and 100 branches; the design should feel capable without overwhelming
5. **Earn trust fast** — Social proof, real numbers, and a low-friction trial are the conversion levers; design should not get in the way

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Body text must hit 4.5:1 contrast. Reduce motion support required for all animations. Spanish-language interface throughout.
